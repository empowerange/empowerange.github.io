Mitti Ke Rang

Mitti Ke Rang is a social venture dedicated to empowering widows and single women to overcome poverty and dependency, through skill-based training that provides employment in the field of Paper and Cloth Bag making. Our eventual goal is to help these women achieve financial stability, self-sufficiency, accountability, and most importantly - to become a role model to their own children, as well as others. ....
